# Achievements

You can earn achievements by completing certain tasks in the game. These will award or penalize points based on the value of a snipe in your game.

## In-Game Achievements

### Automated Achievements
These will be automatically processed and awarded to players

* **Completed Pokedex**
    * Snipe everyone in the game at least once
    * *Snipe Value:* 3
* **Kill Streak**
    * Snipe 5 times in a row without getting sniped
    * *Snipe Value:* 2
* **Shutdown**
    * Snipe someone with a 5+ killstreak, ending their killing spree
    * *Snipe Value:* 1
* **Revengeful**
    * Snipe someone within 7 days of being sniped by them
    * *Snipe Value:* 1
* **Love Triangle**
    * Be in a snipe cycle of 3. All snipes must occur within 7 days
    * *Snipe Value:* 1
* **Nothing Personnel**
    * Snipe the same person at least 3 times
    * *Snipe Value:* 1

### Manual Achievements
These achievements can be granted to players using the `/giveachievement` command

* **Pirate**
    * Snipe while you or the target are on the SeaBus, Aquabus, or any other watercraft.
    * *Snipe Value:* 2
* **Thomas The Tank**
    * Snipe while you or the target are on a train (Skytrain, West Coast Express, etc)
    * *Snipe Value:* 2
* **Sponsored By TransLink**
    * Snipe while you or the target are on a bus. The person sniped does not have to be in the same vehicle
    * *Snipe Value:* 1
* **Screen Peek**
    * Snipe someone after seeing their location from another snipe
    * *Snipe Value:* 0.5
* **Camper**
    * Snipe 3 people from the same location in 3 different photos on the same day
    * *Snipe Value:* 0.5
* **Virtual Insanity**
    * Snipe someone from a video or picture where they are not the focus
    * *Snipe Value:* 0.5
* **Assist Trophy**
    * Snipe someone by getting a picture from an NPC. This snipe does not count for points but grants you this achievement
    * *Snipe Value:* 0.5
* **Ghosts of the Past**
    * Snipe an Alum from your group. This snipe does not count for points but grants you this achievement
    * *Snipe Value:* 0.5
* **Doppelgänger**
    * Have your snipe invalidated due to your target being a doppelgänger
    * *Snipe Value:* -0.5
* **No business on Continental grounds**
    * Have your snipe invalidated due to sniping in a safezone
    * *Snipe Value:* -1

## End of Game Acheivements
These achievements will be automatically processed and awarded using the `/endgame` command. (WIP)

* **Perfectly balanced**
    * End the game with a score of zero. You must have sniped someone and been sniped at least once
    * *Snipe Value:* 2
* **Sitting duck**
    * Be sniped the most times. This is not necessarily the person with the lowest score
    * *Snipe Value:* 2
* **Licence to kill**
    * Snipe the most times. This is not necessarily the person with highest score
    * *Snipe Value:* 1
* **Sneaky Beaky**
    * End the game without ever getting sniped. You must have made at least 3 snipes during the game to earn this
    * *Snipe Value:* 1
* **Pacifist**
    * End the game without sniping anyone
    * *Snipe Value:* 1
